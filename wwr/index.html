Overview

Purpose

We are a rafting tourism company, founded in 2004 by a group of sports and entertainment professionals. Our purpose is to guide you in this wild adventure and help you enjoy the nature to the fullest. We take safety in our activities very seriously. All our guides are trained in the management of leisure activities with groups in Nature.

Audience

If you feel tired of the rutine and you are desperate to try new adventures, we are what you are looking for. All genders, nationalities and ages are welcome to join us and enjoy an out of the ordinary and full of adrenaline adventure, you will enjoy nature to the maximum. Due to security reasons the minimum age to carry out some activities is 12 years.
